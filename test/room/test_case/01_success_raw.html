Description

The Space

Modern and clean 1BR with terrace. Located in the quiet ally between Takeshita street (fashion boutiques) and Omotesandou (Tokyo's highstreet ). 3 minutes from JR Harajuku Station and Subway Meijijingumae Station.

Guest Access

- Modern Entire 1BR with terrace
- Comfortably fits 4 people
- - 1 Queen size bed (160 x 200 cm)
- - 1 Double size sofa bed (140 x 200 cm)
- - 1 Single size floor mattress and comforter (120 x 200 cm) for the extra 5th guest
- Pocket wifi
- Full Kitchenware
- Coffee Maker/Toaster/Microwave/Fridge/Rice cooker
- Full amenities (soap, shampoo, towels, hair dryer, etc)

The Neighborhood

This room gives you privileges to leisurely enjoy Tokyo's central at your own pace. To name a few:
- breakfast on a terrace overlooks boutiques ally and Takeshita street
- visit meijijingu shrine in the morning
- explore Japan's boutiques fashion and indy shops at Takeshita street in the afternoon
- enjoy fine dining and luxury shopping at Omotesandou
- relish one of Tokyo's most dynamic and vibrant nightlife in Shibuya.

Near by Attractions
Takeshita street, Omotesandou street, Jingubashi bridge, Meijijingu shrine, Yoyogi park, Shibuya

Near by Stores
Convenience store 30 secs, 100 yen shop 2 mins, Super market 10 mins
(Please check out guidebook for exact location)

Getting Around

Train (Japan Rail = JR) and subway are the main transportations in Tokyo. The apartment is close to many major lines in Tokyo, Harajuku JR station and Meijijingumae Subway station. Main attractions in Tokyo are accessible within 20 minutes train ride. Many of them are in walking distances.

Nearest Stations (walking)
JR Harajuku station....................... 4 mins
Subway Meijijingumae station....... 3 mins
Subway Omotesando station........ 10 mins

Short train ride from Harajuku station:
Shibuya station........................ 2 mins
Shinjuku station....................... 4 mins
Roppongi station.................... 15 mins
Ginza station.......................... 18 mins

From/To Airport
90 minutes from Narita Airport, take Sky Liner train to Nippori station, then transfer to JR Yamanote line to Harajuku station

40 minutes from Haneda Airport, take Keikyu Line to Shinagawa station, then transfer to JR Yamanote line to Harajuku station

Other Things to Note

- We take cleanliness seriously. Thus, we use professional cleaner for the room
- No extra guest charge for infant
- Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions!
